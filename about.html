About AI & Org, Curated

AI & Org, Curated is a weekly digest exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and organizational design. Technology invents. Organizations innovate. Impact requires both working hand in hand.

Too often, AI breakthroughs are discussed in isolation — new models, new benchmarks, new funding rounds — without asking the harder question: how does any of this translate into real impact inside real organizations? And organizational design conversations rarely account for the capabilities AI is making possible right now.

Each issue is split into two curated halves:

  • AI Brief, Curated — Editorial analysis and hand-picked links covering the most important developments in AI: research, industry moves, policy, and open source.
  • Org Design & Changes, Curated — Essays and insights on how organizations need to evolve — structurally, culturally, and operationally — to turn AI invention into innovation and real impact.

Whether you're a leader scaling a company, a technologist navigating organizational complexity, or someone trying to connect the dots between what AI can do and what organizations actually need — this digest is for you.
