About The AI Brief

The AI Brief is a curated weekly digest that cuts through the noise to bring you the most important developments in artificial intelligence. Every issue combines editorial analysis with a hand-picked selection of links from across the AI landscape.

Each issue includes:

  • Editorial overview — A concise analysis of the week's most significant trends and their implications.
  • Curated links — The best articles, papers, and announcements across research, industry, policy, open source, and more, each with a summary so you can decide what's worth a deeper read.

Whether you're a researcher, engineer, founder, or simply curious about where AI is headed, The AI Brief gives you a clear picture of what happened this week and why it matters — in under 10 minutes.

Published every Friday. Subscribe via RSS to never miss an issue.
